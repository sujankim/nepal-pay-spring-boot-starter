# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers of NepalPay pledge to make participation
in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age,
body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual
identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Use of sexualized language or imagery
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported by contacting the project maintainer at **sujan.officals@email.com**.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response
that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.
